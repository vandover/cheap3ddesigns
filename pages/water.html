Well Sitting Project

Summary:

The Purpose of this project was is to make a device that can read the apparent resistivity of the ground for determining its suitability to be drilled for a water well. The problem is in many third world countries, clean drinking water is only found below the surface of the earth and the equipment for detecting the water is too expensive for the people trying to find water. In countries such as Tanzania the average well is only 30 meters underground where inexpensive hand pumps could be used to bring the water to the surface.

Download Code / Go To GitHub Repository

The project included the following components:

  • Raspberry Pi
  • Relay
  • Rectifier
  • Capacitor
  • Analog to Digital Converter
  • Inverter
  • Car Battery
  • Current and Voltage Sensors
